📖 HOW TO PLAY

GOAL

Checkmate the enemy king. Use gold earned from moves and captures to buy power-ups that shift the odds in your favour.

CHESS RULES

Each player moves one piece per turn (unless BLITZ or EXTRA MOVE is active).

A piece captures by moving onto an enemy square.

You cannot make a move that leaves your king in check.

Castling — king and unmoved rook swap if no pieces between them and neither is in check.

En passant — a pawn that just moved two squares can be captured diagonally by an adjacent enemy pawn on the very next move.

Promotion — when a pawn reaches the far rank, choose queen, rook, bishop, or knight.

TURN STRUCTURE

Make your move on the board.

Optionally buy items from the shop (your turn only).

Click END TURN to pass to your opponent.

A fresh shop rolls automatically at the start of your turn.

BOARD ORIENTATION

VS AI — your pieces are always at the bottom, regardless of colour.

2 PLAYER — the board flips every turn so the active player always faces down.

AI DIFFICULTY

Easy — shallow search, plays randomly among good moves.

Medium — standard minimax, balanced play.

Hard — deeper search, prioritises captures and king safety.

EARNING GOLD

Gold (g) is earned every time you move or capture. Spend it in the shop to buy buffs.

THE SHOP

Each turn you get 3 EQUIPMENT items and 3 EXCLUSIVES drawn from the full pool at random.

Click an item to target a piece (or apply instantly for board effects).

Each item can only be bought once per roll — rerolling restocks all slots.

You can only shop on your own turn.

REROLLING

First reroll costs 10g. Each additional reroll this turn costs +10g more.

The cost resets at the start of your next turn.

COIN BOOST

The COIN BOOST exclusive multiplies all your earnings by ×1.5 for 3 turns. A gold shimmer appears around the board while active.

RESIGN

Click the RESIGN button on your panel at any time to forfeit the game.

BUFF ICONS

Each buff is shown as a small coloured icon in the corner of the piece's square. A coloured border around the square shows the primary buff at a glance. Up to 3 icons are shown per piece.

TIPS

Stack EMPOWER + CAP BONUS on an attacking piece for massive coin bursts.

VENGEANCE on a queen forces opponents to think twice before capturing it.

TRAP TILE on a square your opponent is forced to pass through is devastating.

BLITZ + a well-placed fork can win material in one turn.

FREEZE their queen, then attack around it freely.
